Profile

I am an AI Engineer at SONY, specializing in AI development for the SONY Alpha. Prior to this, I worked as a Research Assistant under Prof. Hiroshi Watanabe, focusing on Image Coding for Machines. My research has been presented at international conferences such as ICIP, MMSP and CCNC.

I earned my B.E. and M.E. degrees from Waseda University, Tokyo, Japan, in 2023 and 2025, respectively. In recognition of my academic achievements, I was honored with both the Department Award and the Major Award upon receiving each degree.
